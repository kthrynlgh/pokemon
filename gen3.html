Hoenn Region

Generation 3

The Hoenn region (ホウエン地方) is a tropical island filled with diverse biomes, including deserts, rainforests, and many islands. It has its own Pokémon League and is home to the legendary Pokémon Groudon and Kyogre, who are said to have formed the land and sea.

キモリ
Treecko

This small, agile Grass-type Pokémon is known for its ability to climb trees and its proud demeanor. It uses its quick speed and agility to battle its opponents.

アチャモ
Torchic

A Fire-type Chick Pokémon, it has a flame sac in its belly that burns continuously. It feels as warm as a hot-water bottle and is known to be very affectionate with its trainer.

ミズゴロウ
Mudkip

This Water/Ground-type Pokémon is known for its powerful mud attacks. It's capable of moving on land more easily than Mudkip, and its strong legs allow it to dig through the mud at high speeds.
