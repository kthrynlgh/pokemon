Generation IV

Sinnoh Region

The Sinnoh Saga

The Sinnoh region (シンオウ地方), is a northern land with diverse landscapes, including sharp peaks, dense woodlands, and picturesque lakes. It is the setting for the games Pokémon Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum.

Generation I

Kanto Region

The Beginning of Adventure

Kanto region (カントー地方) is a diverse area with a temperate climate, featuring a variety of landscapes from small towns and cities to mountains, forests, and seas. It's the setting of the first Pokémon games and is home to Professor Oak and the Kanto Pokémon League.

Generation II

Johto Region

The Journey Continues

The Johto region (ジョウト地方) is a more rural and historical area compared to Kanto, located to its west and sharing the same Pokémon League. It has many old buildings, shrines, and a deep connection to tradition.

Generation III

Hoenn Region

A New World to Explore

The Hoenn region (ホウエン地方) is a tropical island filled with diverse biomes, including deserts, rainforests, and many islands. It has its own Pokémon League and is home to the legendary Pokémon Groudon and Kyogre, who are said to have formed the land and sea.
