Kanto Region

Generation 1

The Kanto region (カントー地方) is a diverse area with a temperate climate, featuring a variety of landscapes from small towns and cities to mountains, forests, and seas. It's the setting of the first Pokémon games and is home to Professor Oak and the Kanto Pokémon League.

フシギダネ
Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type reptilian Pokémon, with a bulb on its back that absorbs sunlight. The bulb contains a seed it uses to launch powerful plant-based attacks.

ヒトカゲ
Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Lizard Pokémon is known for the flame at the tip of its tail, which indicates its health and emotions. If the flame goes out, the Charmander dies.

ゼニガメ
Squirtle

Squirtle is a tiny Water-type Pokémon is a turtle-like creature with a tough shell. Its shell deflects attacks and can be used for a Water-type attack called Rapid Spin.
