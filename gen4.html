Sinnoh region

Generation 4

The Sinnoh region (シンオウ地方), is a northern land with diverse landscapes, including sharp peaks, dense woodlands, and picturesque lakes. It is the setting for the games Pokémon Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum.

ナエトル
Turtwig

A Grass-type Pokémon, it is a small turtle with a shell made of hardened soil. It uses its entire body to photosynthesize when exposed to sunlight, and a moist shell indicates good health.

ヒコザル
Chimchar

This small, monkey-like Fire-type Pokémon has a fiery personality and a flame on its tail. It's known for its playful and energetic nature, and its agility allows it to move with incredible speed.

ポッチャマ
Piplup

This small, penguin-like Water-type Pokémon is known for its prideful nature and its inability to take orders from its trainer. It's a very fast swimmer and can dive to great depths.
