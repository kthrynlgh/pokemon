Johto Region

Generation 2

The Johto region (ジョウト地方) is a more rural and historical area compared to Kanto, located to its west and sharing the same Pokémon League. It has many old buildings, shrines, and a deep connection to tradition.

チコリータ
Chikorita

A small, Grass-type Pokémon with a large leaf on its head, It is known for its friendly demeanor and ability to sense changes in the weather with its leaf.

ヒノアラシ
Cyndaquil

A timid Fire-type Pokémon, It has a flame sac on its back that flares up when it is surprised or angry. It uses these flames to protect itself from attackers.

ワニノコ
Totodile

This small, crocodile-like Water-type Pokémon is known for its playful and mischievous nature. It has a strong jaw that can crush anything it bites down on.
